# Samsung One UI 8.5 Update: Top Features and Improvements

The Samsung One UI 8.5 update brings a host of exciting features and improvements to enhance your mobile experience. One of the standout changes is the revamped user interface, designed to make navigation easier and more intuitive. The updated icon design and layout provide a fresh look, while the enhanced color scheme offers better visual appeal. Additionally, new widget features allow for a more personalized home screen, giving users more control over their device's appearance [1] [citation needed].

## Enhanced User Interface

The One UI 8.5 update also introduces a range of customization options to make your home screen truly unique. With improved icon design and layout, users can easily find what they need. The enhanced color scheme provides a more visually appealing experience, while new widget features enable personalized home screens. These updates make it easier for users to navigate their device and access their favorite features. For instance, the Clock app now supports adding a weather background for daily alarms, providing users with essential information at a glance [1].

## Performance and Battery Life

The One UI 8.5 update also focuses on performance and battery life. Samsung has optimized the system to deliver faster app launch times and overall system performance. This means users can enjoy a seamless experience, with less lag and more efficient performance. Furthermore, the update includes improvements to battery life, with optimized power consumption and enhanced task management for smoother multitasking. According to Samsung, these updates enable users to do more with less effort, featuring upgrades that enable more seamless actions and device management [2] [citation needed][3].

## Camera and Media Features

The One UI 8.5 update brings a range of exciting camera and media features. New camera modes have been added to improve photo and video capture, giving users more creative control. Enhanced image editing capabilities also allow for more precise adjustments and effects. Additionally, the update supports new media formats and playback features, making it easier to enjoy your favorite content on your device. With One UI 8.5, creating and sharing content becomes more intuitive, from start to finish [2].

## Security and Accessibility

Security and accessibility are also key areas of focus in the One UI 8.5 update. Samsung has enhanced biometric security features, providing users with more secure and convenient ways to protect their device. Improved accessibility options are also included, making it easier for users with disabilities to navigate and use their device. Regular security updates and patches ensure that users' devices stay protected and up-to-date [1].

## Compatibility and Rollout

The One UI 8.5 update will be rolled out to a range of Samsung devices. According to Samsung's usual firmware update policy, devices such as the Galaxy S25 Ultra, Galaxy S25 Edge, Galaxy Z Fold 7, and Galaxy Z Flip 7 will receive the update [6] [citation needed][7] [citation needed]. The first beta firmware will be available in Korea, Germany, the US, and the UK, with India and Poland joining at a later date [5]. The expected rollout timeline varies by region, but users can expect to receive the update in the coming weeks. To update to One UI 8.5, users can check for software updates in their device's settings app. For those eager to try the beta version, Samsung has launched a beta program in select countries, with the second beta expected to arrive on December 22, 2025 [4] [citation needed].
